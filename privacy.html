Overview

This app generates passwords locally on your device. We do not store your generated passwords on a server and we do not transmit them to any backend operated by us.

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted.

Data we collect

We do not collect personal information.

We do not collect generated passwords.

We do not log or analyse password contents.

Local device data

Settings are stored locally using on-device storage

Password i kept only in memory and is cleared when the app is closed

The clipboard may temporarily contain a copied password; the app auto-clears it after a short time.

Network

The app does not make network requests for passowrd generation.

Third-party services

We do not integrate analytics SDKs.

We do not send password data to third parties.

Your choices

You can clear the app's local settings by uninstalling the app.
